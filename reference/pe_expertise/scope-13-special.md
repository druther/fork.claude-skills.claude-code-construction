# Scope File: Division 13 — Special Construction

**Load when:** Query references swimming pools, clean rooms, recording studios, radiation shielding, vaults, pre-engineered metal buildings, seismic isolation, or any spec section 13 XX 00.

**Red flags and coordination matrix are in pe_behavior.md (always loaded).**

---

## Cross-Reference Triggers

- Project-specific — identify ALL Division 13 sections in the spec table of contents (this division varies radically by project type).
- Structural plans — special loading, vibration isolation, shielding requirements.
- MEP plans — specialized systems (pool mechanical, clean room HVAC, studio isolation, lab gases).
- Specialty consultant drawings (verify these are included in the document set).
- Division 01 — delegated design requirements for pre-engineered systems.
- **Submittal register** — check for approved specialty system submittals, particularly delegated design calculations.
- **RFI log** — check for RFIs about specialty system interfaces or performance requirements.

---

## Absence Detection Checklist

- [ ] Specialty consultant drawings referenced AND included in the document set.
- [ ] Performance specifications for specialized environments (clean room class, STC/IIC, pool water chemistry, radiation shielding thickness).
- [ ] Interface details between special construction and standard building assemblies.
- [ ] Delegated design submittals with EOR review (pre-engineered structures, seismic isolation).
- [ ] Specialized MEP systems designed and coordinated (pool filtration, clean room air handling, studio HVAC isolation).
- [ ] Specialty testing and commissioning requirements defined.

---

## Sequencing Context

Division 13 items are project-specific. The universal rule:

```
Identify ALL Division 13 scope early in preconstruction
  → POINT OF NO RETURN: Specialty systems often require structural accommodations
    ├── Pool shells require structural design for hydrostatic loads
    ├── Clean rooms require envelope-within-envelope framing
    ├── Recording studios require isolated structural slabs (floating floors)
    ├── Radiation shielding requires specific wall thickness/material at framing stage
    └── Pre-engineered buildings require foundation design coordination
  → Specialty consultant involvement in submittals and inspections
  → Specialty commissioning (often separate from building commissioning)
```

**Critical rule:** Division 13 items are the most likely to be overlooked in coordination because they are unfamiliar to the standard trade contractors. Verify early that someone on the project team has experience with the specific specialty system.

---

## Coordination Overlaps

- **With Structural (Div 03/05):** Special loading, vibration isolation, structural separation.
- **With MEP (Div 21–26):** Specialized MEP systems often have different standards than building systems.
- **With Division 01:** Delegated design responsibility and specialty consultant coordination.
