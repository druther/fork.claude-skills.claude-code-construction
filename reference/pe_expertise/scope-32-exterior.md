# Scope File: Division 32 — Exterior Improvements

**Load when:** Query references paving, sidewalks, curbing, landscape, irrigation, fencing, site amenities, pavement markings, parking, or any spec section 32 XX 00.

**Red flags and coordination matrix are in pe_behavior.md (always loaded).**

---

## Cross-Reference Triggers

- Civil site plans — paving layout, grading, sidewalks, curbing, ADA route.
- Landscape plans — planting, irrigation, site furnishings.
- Spec 32 10 00 — Bases, Ballasts, and Paving.
- Spec 32 12 00 — Flexible Paving (asphalt).
- Spec 32 13 00 — Rigid Paving (concrete sidewalks, curbing).
- Spec 32 17 00 — Paving Specialties (markings, signage, speed bumps, bollards).
- Spec 32 31 00 — Fences and Gates.
- Spec 32 84 00 — Planting Irrigation.
- Spec 32 90 00 — Planting.
- Spec 32 93 00 — Plants (trees, shrubs, groundcover, sod/seed).
- ADA — accessible route from parking to entry, slope requirements, detectable warnings.
- Division 31 — Subgrade preparation under paving.
- Division 33 — Utility locations under paving and landscape areas.
- Division 26 — Exterior/site lighting at parking and pedestrian areas.
- **Submittal register** — check for approved paving mix designs, plant material, and irrigation system submittals.
- **RFI log** — check for RFIs about ADA route, grading, or utility conflicts under paving.

---

## Absence Detection Checklist

- [ ] ADA accessible route from accessible parking to building entrance(s). (pe_behavior.md Red Flag §4.6)
- [ ] Detectable warning surfaces (truncated domes) at all curb ramps.
- [ ] Pavement cross-slopes ≤ 2% per ADA.
- [ ] Accessible parking quantity, size, signage, and access aisle per code. (pe_behavior.md Red Flag §4.6)
- [ ] Pavement section details (subgrade, base course, wearing course) with thicknesses.
- [ ] Concrete sidewalk thickness, reinforcement, and joint spacing.
- [ ] Irrigation coverage matching planting plan.
- [ ] Site lighting at all parking and pedestrian areas.
- [ ] Stormwater management (bioswales, retention/detention, permeable paving).
- [ ] Approved paving and landscape submittals (check register).

## Sequencing Context

```
Building construction substantially complete
  → Underground utilities complete in paving areas (Div 33)
  → POINT OF NO RETURN: All underground work BEFORE paving
    ├── Utility trenches backfilled and compacted
    ├── Site lighting conduit and bases installed
    ├── Irrigation main lines installed
    └── Any work requiring excavation in paved areas must be done BEFORE paving
  → Subgrade preparation and compaction
  → Base course
  → Paving (asphalt or concrete)
  → Curbing and sidewalks
  → Pavement markings (striping, signage)
  → Landscape installation (typically last site activity — plant material is perishable)
  → POINT OF NO RETURN: Planting season
    └── Most planting has seasonal windows (spring/fall in most climates)
    └── Missing the planting window = temporary stabilization and deferred planting
```

---

## Coordination Overlaps

- **With Earthwork (Div 31):** Subgrade preparation under paving.
- **With Utilities (Div 33):** All underground work complete before paving. Utility locations under landscape areas.
- **With Electrical (Div 26):** Site lighting conduit and foundations.
- **With Plumbing (Div 22):** Irrigation water supply connection.
