# Scope File: Division 01 — General Requirements

**Load when:** Query references project-wide procedures, submittals, closeout, temporary facilities, allowances, alternates, substitutions, phasing, or any specification section 01 XX 00. Also load when any other scope query requires verification of project-wide administrative rules.

**Red flags and coordination matrix are in pe_behavior.md (always loaded). This file provides detailed cross-reference lists and absence checklists.**

---

## Cross-Reference Triggers

When a query involves project administration, submittal procedures, quality control, temporary facilities, or closeout, pull ALL of the following before responding:

- Spec 01 10 00 — Summary of Work: project description, Owner's work, separate contracts, contractor use of premises, phasing.
- Spec 01 20 00 — Price and Payment Procedures: unit prices, allowances, alternates.
- Spec 01 25 00 — Substitution Procedures: pre-bid vs. post-bid rules, documentation requirements.
- Spec 01 30 00 — Administrative Requirements: coordination, meetings, submittal procedures.
- Spec 01 33 00 — Submittal Procedures: shop drawings, product data, samples, mock-ups, delegated design.
- Spec 01 40 00 — Quality Requirements: testing/inspection responsibilities, tolerances, mock-ups, special inspections.
- Spec 01 50 00 — Temporary Facilities and Controls: temp power, water, heat, enclosures, protection, environmental controls.
- Spec 01 60 00 — Product Requirements: delivery/storage/handling, product options and substitutions.
- Spec 01 70 00 — Execution and Closeout: cutting/patching, cleaning, waste, closeout, O&M, warranties, as-builts.
- Spec 01 73 00 — Execution: field engineering, surveying, preparation, installation tolerances.
- Spec 01 77 00 — Closeout Procedures: substantial completion, final completion, final cleaning, warranty start dates.
- Spec 01 78 00 — Closeout Submittals: O&M data, warranties, project record documents.
- General Conditions — identify edition (AIA A201, ConsensusDocs, or custom); check for modifications in Supplementary Conditions.
- Supplementary Conditions — modifications to General Conditions, insurance, bonds, retainage, liquidated damages.
- Drawing sheet G-001/G-002 — general notes, abbreviations, symbols, drawing index.
- **Submittal register** — verify submittal review periods match Spec 01 33 00 requirements.
- **RFI log** — check for any RFIs about project procedures, phasing, or administrative requirements.

## Absence Detection Checklist

- [ ] Summary of Work section identifying all work by separate contracts or by Owner.
- [ ] Submittal procedures with defined review periods and re-submittal process.
- [ ] Allowance schedule with defined values and procedures for expenditure.
- [ ] Alternates schedule with defined add/deduct scope for each alternate.
- [ ] Cutting and patching responsibility matrix or written designation.
- [ ] Warranty duration schedule (which warranties exceed the standard 1-year).
- [ ] Substantial completion criteria and punchlist procedures.
- [ ] Phasing or sequencing requirements if project is multi-phase.
- [ ] Special inspection schedule identifying required inspections and responsible parties.
- [ ] Mock-up requirements identified (which systems, when, approval criteria).
- [ ] Temporary facilities responsibilities (who provides temp power, water, heat, hoisting).
- [ ] Protection of finished work responsibilities during subsequent trades' installation.
- [ ] As-built documentation requirements (format, frequency of updates, final submission).
- [ ] Commissioning agent scope and authority defined (if applicable).

## Sequencing Context

Division 01 governs the ENTIRE project timeline. Key sequencing dependencies:

**Pre-Construction:**
1. Identify all required submittals per Spec 01 33 00 and build submittal register.
2. Identify all required mock-ups per Spec 01 40 00 — mock-ups must be approved before production work.
3. Identify phasing requirements per Spec 01 10 00 — affects every trade's sequencing.

**During Construction:**
- Submittal review periods (typically 10–14 business days per review cycle) are on the critical path for procurement. A submittal rejected and requiring re-submission adds another full review cycle.
- **POINT OF NO RETURN:** Substitution requests must be submitted per Spec 01 25 00 timelines. Post-bid substitutions have different (usually more restrictive) requirements than pre-bid.

**Closeout:**
- Spec 01 77 00/01 78 00 define the substantial completion and final completion sequence.
- O&M manuals and warranties must be assembled BEFORE substantial completion.
- **POINT OF NO RETURN:** Warranty start dates are typically tied to substantial completion date. Missing this date affects all warranty durations.

## Coordination Overlaps

- **With ALL Divisions:** Division 01 requirements apply globally. Cutting and patching (01 73 00), submittal procedures (01 33 00), and quality requirements (01 40 00) affect every trade.
- **With Division 07/09:** Mock-up requirements for exterior envelope, interior finishes.
- **With MEP (Divisions 21–28):** Commissioning scope, TAB requirements, special inspections.
- **Scope boundary (pe_behavior.md §7.1):** Cutting and patching responsibility is the most commonly disputed Division 01 item. Verify explicit assignment.
